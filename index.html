BA Chinese Studies and MA International and Asian Studies Graduate

About me:

I am a graduate student at the University of Nottingham. My graduate work focuses on International Relations specifically with interest in Asian global affairs, targetting countries such as China, South Korea and Japan. At the undergraduate level I studied Chinese language and culture graduating with a first-class degree (w/honours) and accolades including an excellence award. In order to promote an easier transition for Chinese students studying in the UK I was a founder and the first president of the Chinese Studies society at the University of Hull. On top of this I spent a total of two years in China, one year in Heilongjiang (黑龙江) teaching English as a volunteer, and another year in Beijing (北京) as part of a year abroad programme for my Bachelors Degree at Beijing Language and Culture University.
